# Robo Sumo Bot

A simple, 3D printable robo sumo bot designed for educational purposes and robotics competitions.

## Features

- Fully 3D printable chassis
- Designed for 12V DC gear motors
- Supports a dual-channel ESC
- Compatible with the FlySky FS-i6 transmitter and receiver
- Powered by a 3S LiPo battery (11.1V nominal)

## Required Components

- 3D printed parts
- 2 × 12V DC gear motors
- 1 × Dual-channel ESC
- 1 × FlySky FS-i6 transmitter
- 1 × FlySky receiver
- 1 × 3S LiPo battery
- Wheels
- M3 screws and nuts


## Printing

Recommended settings:

- Material: PLA or PETG
- Layer height: 0.2 mm
- Infill: 30%
- Supports: Only where required
